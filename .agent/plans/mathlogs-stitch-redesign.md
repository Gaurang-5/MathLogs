# MathLogs Landing Page - Structure & Redesign Brief

This document provides a comprehensive breakdown of the current MathLogs landing page, along with specific UI/UX directives to guide the redesign process.

## 1. Project Context & Target Audience
* **Product Name:** MathLogs
* **Niche:** Coaching management platform tailored for single-teacher centres.
* **Core Value Proposition:** Spend less time on paperwork. More time teaching. Manage batches, fees, tests, and automated WhatsApp alerts without complex software.
* **Target Audience:** Individual educators and coaching centre owners who currently rely on physical registers, Excel sheets, and manual WhatsApp messaging to manage 100+ students. They value simplicity, speed, and reliability.

## 3. Mandatory Redesign Directives (For the UX/UI Designer)

To ensure MathLogs stands out and avoids looking like a "generic SaaS template," please adhere strictly to these design constraints:

### Topology & Layout (Anti-Safe Harbor)
* **NO Standard Splits:** Do **not** use the cliché "Text on Left, Image on Right" hero layouts. 
* **NO Default Grids:** Avoid predictable Bento box grids for feature sections unless the data complexity strictly demands it.
* **Alternative Approaches:** Explore Fragmented layouts, Layered Depth (Z-axis overlap), extreme Asymmetry (e.g., 90/10 split), or Massive Typographic Heroes. Force visual differentiation.

### Color Palette (The Purple Ban)
* **Forbidden Colors:** Do NOT use Purple, Violet, Indigo, or Magenta. These are overused "AI/Tech" clichés.
* **Avoid "Fintech Blue":** Move away from safe, generic SaaS blues. 
* **Required Palette:** Choose bold, high-contrast, memorable colors. Consider Acid Green, Signal Orange, Deep Red, or high-contrast Monochrome palettes that evoke efficiency, trust, and speed.

### Geometry & Styling
* **Avoid the "Safe Middle":** Do not default to `6px - 8px` rounded corners for everything. 
* **Make a Statement:** Go extreme. Use either sharp, brutalist corners (`0px - 2px`) for a technical, crisp feel OR fully organic pill shapes (`16px - 32px`). Be intentional with edges.
* **No Glass-trap / Soft-glows:** Avoid generic backdrop-blur and soft multi-colored background auroras. Use raw, solid borders and high-contrast visuals or grain textures instead.

### Motion & Visual Depth
* **Static is Failure:** The design must account for staggered scroll-reveals and active micro-interactions (e.g., scale or pulse on hover). 
* **Layering:** Create depth with overlapping UI elements and parallax layers, rather than relying on flat, single-plane designs.
